Intelligent Compression

Reduce file sizes by up to 60% with smart compression. Preview before and after, compare quality side-by-side, and choose how to save.

Crop & Resize

Perfect aspect ratios for Instagram, TikTok, and more. Rotate, crop, and resize with preset or custom dimensions.

Smart Filters

Filter by file type, size, screenshots, favorites, or date. Find that 1GB video eating your storage in seconds.

Batch Operations

Select multiple photos with drag gestures and delete, compress, or share them all at once. Clean hundreds of photos in minutes.
